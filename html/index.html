Noakhali, a coastal district in Bangladesh, often faces severe flooding, especially during monsoon seasons and cyclones. Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement

The Quota Reform Movement in Bangladesh was a significant student-led protest that took place in 2018, primarily demanding reforms in the government job quota system. The movement sought to reduce the percentage of jobs reserved for specific groups, arguing that the system was unfairly limiting opportunities for merit-based candidates.
